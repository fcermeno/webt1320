Curaçao is in the Southern Caribbean Sea north of the Venezuelan coast, together with sister islands Aruba and Bonaire making them the ABC islands. Savor new flavors with a late-night bite at any food truck favorites, get lost in paradisiac beaches like Kenepa Chiki ,or take on new terrain with a hike through Christoffel Park.

Curaçao's Historic Waterfront

The Handelskade is the most breathtaking part of Punda, Curaçao. This historic waterfront strip is a vibrant reminder of Curaçao's Dutch heritage, as well as a favorite #instaworthy for photography. The famous colors of the Handelskade remain eye-candy, especially by night.

Curacao nightlife

The night vibrates every day in this island! It all starts early in the evening at the bar & restaurants with live music. After it relocates to the nearby Beach and continues deep into the night. Don't be shy and dance, let yourself go!

Nature and Wildlife

One of the colorful atractions in Curacao is their Wildlife. One of the best atractions is the Flamingo Sanctuary Sint Willibrordus, were you will find many of this feather friends socializing surrounded by calm waters and spring hills.

Snorkeling at Tugboat

The Tugboat is the most popular snorkeling spot in Curacao. This cool spot is sheltered in a shallow bay on the island’s southwestern coast. Covered by corals, sponges and sea fans, the tugboat protects a mix of colorful reef fish.
